You're in

You're Pulse People now.

A few small things keep these places good for everyone. That's the whole deal.

1
Claim what you'll keep.
A seat you hold is one nobody else can. Only claim the nights you mean to show up for.
2
Running late? You've got 20.
Twenty minutes' grace at the door, every time. A quick heads-up keeps your seat warm.
3
Show up to be counted.
Checking in from inside the room — not the sidewalk — is what makes a night hum. Presence, not a fly-by.
4
Plans change — free the seat early.
Cancel ahead and it finds someone who wanted it. Show up for the rooms that show up for you, and Pulse surfaces you first.
