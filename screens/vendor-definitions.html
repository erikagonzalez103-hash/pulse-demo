What the numbers mean

Plain definitions for the words across your dashboard and analytics.
Momentum
Your venue's live signal. Built only from real check-ins — never from posting an offer — and it's self-relative: you against your own past by day-part, never ranked against other venues.
Claimed
A guest has held a seat under your offer's terms. A promise to come — not money in the door, and not yet an arrival.
Checked-in
A guest actually arrived and checked in from inside your place. This is the one that moves your momentum — attendance, not intent.
Saved
A guest bookmarked you for later. A signal of interest — not a commitment, and it doesn't move momentum on its own.
Slipping
A night running behind your own norm for that day-part. Shows in amber (never red) and always comes with honest levers you control.
